# GamePlay
Your character is spawned in at the center of the map, with some cover. The player can move by:
 1. use **WASD** to move Left, Right, Forward and Backwards.
 2. holding **Shift** makes the player move faster in any direction
 3. pressing the **Space Bar** makes the player character jump

 Your character also has to defend themselves from waves of enemies on all sides. These waves get more and more intense with each beaten wave, with more amounts of enemies spawning in. Enemies chase after you until your within range and attack. the enemies are:

 1. Deathclaw: very strong and fast, which deal an immense amount of damage and has a lot of health. This enemy only begins to appear in the late game.
 2. Assaultron: has 2 phases depending on closeness. If a player is within a certain range of the player it will run towards them and melee, if not they will start to shoot at them. fast with a medium amount of health.
 3. power armour: stands and shoots, a more powerful version of a basic troop
 4. NCR Ranger: stands and shoots when within a certain range of the player, shoots a certain amount of time then reloads

 The player defends themselves using a pistol controlled by the mouse, also how the player looks around. The controls for the gun are:
 1. **Left Click** to shoot the gun
 2. **Right CLick** to zoom in
 3. **Scroll Bar** or **R** to reload the weapon
